§46 — The Future Tense

Conjugate each verb in the future tense. Remember: regular verbs attach endings directly to the infinitive. Irregular verbs use a modified stem.

Regular endings: -é, -ás, -á, -emos, -éis, -án
Irregular stems: dir-, habr-, har-, podr-, pondr-, querr-, sabr-, saldr-, tendr-, vendr-

§47 — Subjunctive After Conjunctions of Time

Choose or conjugate the verb using the subjunctive (future/pending event) or indicative (habitual/past). Antes (de) que always takes subjunctive.

Future/Pending → Subjunctive  |  Habitual/Past → Indicative
antes (de) que → always subjunctive
Conjunctions: antes (de) que, cuando, después (de) que, en cuanto, hasta que, tan pronto como

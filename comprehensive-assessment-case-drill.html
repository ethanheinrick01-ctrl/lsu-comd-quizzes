Norms Strip

Degree: 0-25 WNL, 26-40 mild, 41-55 moderate, 56-70 mod-severe, 71-90 severe, 91+ profound.
SRT-PTA: agreement is within +/-10 dB. Larger gaps need an explanation.
WRS: 90-100 WNL, 75-90 slight, 60-75 moderate, 50-60 poor, under 50 very poor.
Type B + ECV: normal = middle ear pathology; large = perforation/patent tube; small = occlusion/probe issue.
Reflex SL: reduced points cochlear/recruitment; absent often conductive barrier; elevated can be conductive or retrocochlear.
Cross-check: do not diagnose from one measure. Make every measure fit the same site of lesion.
